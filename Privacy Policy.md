# Privacy Policy for Dvait (Beta)

**Last Updated:** March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dvait ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Dvait is designed as a privacy-first, local AI memory agent.

## 1. Use of AccessibilityService API
Dvait explicitly requires the use of the Android `AccessibilityService` API to function. 
* **What data is collected:** The app uses the AccessibilityService API to read and capture the text content displayed on your screen across various apps.
* **How the data is used:** This text is processed to build a contextual memory graph, allowing the app to extract important tasks, schedule items, and notes to power your intelligent home screen widget.
* **Data Transmission and Storage:** In this Beta version, all captured screen data is stored **strictly locally** on your device within a secure database. If you utilize the "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) feature, text snippets are routed directly to the third-party LLM provider you configure (e.g., Groq) and are subject to their privacy policies. We do not transmit, upload, or store your screen data on our servers.

## 2. Data Sharing and Sales
**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or screen content with data brokers, advertising networks, or any third parties.**

## 3. User Control and Data Deletion
Because your data is stored locally, you maintain absolute control. You can instantly delete all captured memory data by clearing the app's storage in your Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

## 4. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the open-source nature of this application, please open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
